Blanchard Piano

Tuning, Regulation, Repair, Restoration

Blanchard Piano Service is a multi-generational piano tuning and restoration company owned and operated by Michael Blanchard.

With over twenty years in the trade, Michael has extensive experience working on all models of pianos, foreign and domestic. All aspects of tuning are done entirely by ear, absent of the use of any electronic tuning devices or equipment. Blanchard Piano provides piano technology services for numerous academic institutions, religious organizations and private clientele throughout all of New England.

Contact us today at +1 (978) 785-9434.

REGULATION

Complete or partial regulation to keep your piano playing at its best.

REPAIR

Repairs of all foreign and domestic pianos. Ivory repairs to complete action overhauls.

RESTORATION

Complete or partial restoration and refinishing. Small restorations to entire re-manufacturing of all foreign and domestic pianos from any vintage.

TUNING

All tunings are done by ear. No tuning devices or electronics are ever used.
